To this piece of impudence, cruelty and affectation, I could reply only by ejaculating the monosyllable "Help" "Elp" echoed the ruffian "not I. Dare iz te pottle elp yourself, und pe tam'd" With these words he let fall a heavy bottle of Kirschenwasser which, dropping precisely upon the crown of my head, caused me to imagine that my brains were entirely knocked out.
