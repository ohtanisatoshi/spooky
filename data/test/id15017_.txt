When called upon to play Thomas Hawk, I anointed a sheet of fools cap with the white of a gander's egg; then, shredding the thing to be reviewed as I had previously shredded the books, only with more care, so as to get every word separate I threw the latter shreds in with the former, screwed on the lid of the castor, gave it a shake, and so dusted out the mixture upon the egg'd foolscap; where it stuck.
