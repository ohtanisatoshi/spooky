Madman" here he sprang furiously to his feet, and shrieked out his syllables, as if in the effort he were giving up his soul "Madman I tell you that she now stands without the door" As if in the superhuman energy of his utterance there had been found the potency of a spell the huge antique pannels to which the speaker pointed, threw slowly back, upon the instant, their ponderous and ebony jaws.
