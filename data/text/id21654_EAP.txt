I was hastening to anticipate a second disturbance, when a page of Mentoni's household burst into the room, and faltered out, in a voice choking with emotion, the incoherent words, "My mistress my mistress Poisoned poisoned Oh, beautiful oh, beautiful Aphrodite" Bewildered, I flew to the ottoman, and endeavored to arouse the sleeper to a sense of the startling intelligence.
