"The balloon, then, had actually burst" These were the first tumultuous ideas that hurried through my mind: "The balloon had positively burst I was falling falling with the most impetuous, the most unparalleled velocity To judge by the immense distance already so quickly passed over, it could not be more than ten minutes, at the farthest, before I should meet the surface of the earth, and be hurled into annihilation" But at length reflection came to my relief.
