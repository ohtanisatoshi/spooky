Looking timidly around, as if she feared to be overheard, she whispered: "I have discovered to morrow that is, to day already the to morrow is come before dawn, foreigners, Austrians, my mother's hirelings, are to carry me off to Germany, to prison, to marriage to anything, except you and my brother take me away, or soon they will be here" I was frightened by her vehemence, and imagined some mistake in her incoherent tale; but I no longer hesitated to obey her.
